Juniors Are Awesome (JAA)

We are a support group for junior developers and newcomers to tech.

I know juniors are awesome. Tell me more!

Organizations like Rails Girls have done a great job of helping beginners discover the world of coding. But once you get there, then what?

Many people leave tech because they are discouraged by the atmosphere, lack of support etc... Unfortunately, we've found that many people don't talk about this until AFTER they've left.

Juniors Are Awesome (JAA) aims to be a safe and confidential space where junior developers & newbies can find support, ask for help, and find mentors among OTHER juniors. Because who knows better what you're going through than someone who is in the same position?

How can I join?

At the moment we exist as an invite-only slack channel. If you are interested in joining, we will ask you to fill out a form so that we can determine whether or not you fit the bill. Requirements are:

  1. You have read and agree to our Code of Conduct
  2. You are trustworthy
  3. You are a junior/beginner in the tech world
  4. You are not the boss of a tech company
  5. You are not the immediate supervisor of a current JAA member
Apply to join!

But I'm a senior, what can I do?

We treasure your input and knowledge, and would love for you to share it with us. If you have some ideas about how you or your company can/do help juniors, we definitely want to hear from you. For example:

  1. onboarding ideas
  2. mentoring
  3. feedback sessions
  4. etc ...

We will share your input within our network of juniors. We will get back to you if we have any questions.

Share your knowledge!

JAA is a group created & moderated by Tam (@travelingtamm) & Sara (@sareg0), inspired by WAAA.

Feel free to contact us via email if you have any questions!
