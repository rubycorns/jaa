Juniors Are Awesome (JAA)

We are a support group for junior developers and newcomers to tech.

We are no longer taking applications for the JAA Slack

Running the channel was super awesome, but we'd like to focus our time and effort on other things for now. Thank you JAA-bers who supported us and made the channel wonderful while it lasted. Your contributions, stories and love helped make it a very special place to be.

Here are some places you might want to go to if you're looking for a supporting community as a Junior Software Developer: Code Newbie The Practical Dev

Thank you to everyone who helped JAA be a thing for a while. We're extremely grateful for your advice and efforts and enthusiasm. !()[https://media.giphy.com/media/144Gf2oA74mHQc/giphy.gif]

JAA is a group created & moderated by Tam (@travelingtamm) & Sara (@sareg0), inspired by WAAA.

Feel free to contact us via email if you have any questions!
